First off, What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a digital currency with no bills to print or coins to mint that was created by an unknow person or group called Satoshi Nakamoto.For Bitcoin there is not government or bank that controls the money.The owners are anonymous to where they don't use their names,tax IDs, or social security numbers instead the system uses encryption keys to buy or sell.
